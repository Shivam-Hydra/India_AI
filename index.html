How SARTHI Works

Experience seamless heritage exploration with our intuitive step-by-step process

Entering a Monument

As you approach a heritage site, SARTHI automatically detects your location using geofencing technology and downloads the site-specific information pack.

Guided Experience

You're welcomed with a personalized voice message and presented with a detailed map showing structures, paths, viewpoints, and facilities.

Cultural Narration

As you walk through the site, SARTHI automatically triggers location-based audio narratives about architecture, history, and cultural significance.

Safety Monitoring

The app constantly monitors for overcrowding and restricted zones, sending gentle alerts if you approach protected or hazardous areas.

Emergency Support

If you need help, the SOS button immediately shares your location with authorities and provides self-help instructions until assistance arrives.

Interactive Learning

Ask questions by voice, take cultural quizzes, explore lesser-known stories, and get personalized recommendations for further exploration.

Freemium Access Model

  • Free Tier: Basic cultural stories, navigation assistance, rule awareness, and safety alerts available to all users
  • Premium Tier: Advanced features including exclusive cultural tours, AR experiences, in-depth storytelling, and personalized itineraries
  • Enterprise Tier: Custom dashboard access for heritage site authorities and tourism departments

Diverse Revenue Streams

  • Tourism Board Partnerships: Collaboration with state and national tourism departments
  • Dashboard Licensing: Customized management dashboards for heritage site authorities
  • Artisan Marketplace Commissions: Percentage from sales through ONDC integration
  • Premium Subscriptions: Monthly or annual subscriptions for advanced features

Impact Potential

  • Heritage Preservation: Reduced monument damage, better conservation awareness
